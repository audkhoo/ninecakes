About Us

9 Cakes Patisserie is a homestyle bakery run by a small team of dedicated and passionate bakers. We specialise in soft and light cream cakes, suitable for all your special occasions. All our cakes are made-to-order to ensure freshness and quality, and we only serve what we love to eat. Just like baking for our family and friends, we bake with love. Our cakes have all been meticulously tested out and are lower in sugar, soft, fluffy and fresh, suitable for the young and old as we use all natural products without preservatives and all cakes are made fresh to order. More cake, less guilt! Hooray!

Our Vision

We want to provide quality and delicious cakes and desserts to everyone. All our cakes are freshly baked to order, using only the finest quality ingredients, we bake light and fluffy cakes. Creating aesthetically looking cakes with delightful flavours that bring family and friends closer with every slice. All the bakes are baked fresh in small batches upon orders. We are constantly on the lookout for exciting and delicious bakes, so there is always something to look forward to for everyone. We understand that there are no shortcuts in life and that applies to our philosophy in baking too. Each cake & treat is baked and hand decorated with humble ingredients without any preservatives. Let us deliver sweetness in your lives. To date, we have served over 20,000 customers and have been featured on Times Magazine and the Straits Times.
